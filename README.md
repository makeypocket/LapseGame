# LAPSE GAME 2075

[![Built with Pollinations](https://img.shields.io/badge/Built%20with-Pollinations-8a2be2?style=for-the-badge&logo=data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns%3D%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox%3D%220%200%20124%20124%22%3E%3Ccircle%20cx%3D%2262%22%20cy%3D%2262%22%20r%3D%2262%22%20fill%3D%22%23ffffff%22/%3E%3C/svg%3E&logoColor=white&labelColor=6a0dad)](https://pollinations.ai)

Test yourself as a president in a crazy world! **Lapse Game 2075** is an AI-powered decision-making game inspired by the "Reigns" series, where every choice you make impacts the future of your country across four key pillars: Environment, People, Military, and Economy.

![Pollinations Logo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pollinations/pollinations/main/assets/logo-text.svg)

## ðŸŒŸ Features

- **AI-Driven Storytelling**: Powered by [pollinations.ai](https://pollinations.ai), the game generates unique scenarios and consequences on the fly.
- **Dynamic Decision Making**: Swipe or click to make choices that will lead your nation to prosperity or ruin.
- **Multiple AI Models**: Choose between various LLMs (Gemini, GPT-5, Grok, Qwen) to change the "personality" of your game master.
- **Perk System**: Unlock special abilities like "Economic Boost" or "Military Reserve" to save your run when stats hit critical levels.
- **Multilingual Support**: Play in either English or Russian.
